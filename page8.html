Automatic Solutions Iberia attended the Annual Dealer Meeting that Automated Logic organized in Cancun from February 18th to 20th

In a relaxed work environment and in a privileged environment, ALC presented the new products. Within the Carrier group, Automated Logic is driving the innovation of building solutions. As we could see in detail with OPTIFLEX Virtual Integrator, an integration platform of up to 50,000 BACnet / IP and Modbus TCP / IP points that managed by WebCTROL offer enormous savings in hardware costs.

In addition to speedboat rides and enjoying the local climate and cuisine, it was a joy to meet the extensive family of Automated Logic around the world with more than 400 dealers.

Training in Barcelona

After the summer of 18 we have been with a workshop composed by Markus Gebele, Regional Manager Central Europe
AutomatedLogic Corporation and Automatic Solutions Iberia technicians to go into detail and get to know WebControl news better and better.

Constitution of the company

Automatic Solutions Iberia celebrated its constitution with its highest represented in Europe Mr. Dave Wijn- Vice President Sales Europe and Caecilie Olive Hechtel Sales Director South Europe both of Automated Logic. The European product innovations were presented and the business and operations managers of Automatic Solutions Iberia were able to update, strengthen our ties in a relaxed work environment.
